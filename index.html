What is Jiffy?

Jiffy is a smart tracking sticker that helps people locate misplaced items quickly and easily. Simply attach a Jiffy sticker to your belongings and find them using sound, light, or the Jiffy mobile app.

Our Product

The Jiffy Smart Sticker is lightweight, unique designs, durable and easy to attach. Once connected, you can instantly make it ring or flash when an item goes missing. No more wasted time and no more stress.
